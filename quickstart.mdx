## Stop Clicking, Start Delegating

Getting started with Anyx WorkOS is radically different from traditional SaaS tools. You don't need to learn a new interface or configure complex boards. You just need to connect WhatsApp and start talking.

---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your WhatsApp">
    Go to [anyx.app](https://anyx.app) and sign in.
    
    In the main view, you will be prompted to link your WhatsApp number. This creates your secure, isolated communication tunnel to **RawBot**, your new 1-on-1 executive assistant.
    
    <Tip>
      Ensure you are using your primary WhatsApp number. Our Zero-Knowledge architecture ensures your private data never leaves your isolated environment.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Your First Message">
    Once connected, you will receive an automatic WhatsApp message from your agent.
    
    Don't think of it as "prompt engineering." Think of it as leaving a voice note for a very capable employee.
    
    **Try sending:**
    ```
    "Hey RawBot, I'm Michael. I want to build a quick daily habit. Every morning at 8:00 AM, send me a brief summary of the most important tech news from today so I can read it with my coffee."
    ```
    
    RawBot will automatically generate a **Proactive Rule** in the background. Tomorrow at 8:00 AM, you'll receive your briefing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Your Stack (Optional but Recommended)">
    To unlock the true power of WorkOS, you need to give RawBot access to your tools.
    
    1. Navigate to the **Integration Hub** on your Anyx Dashboard.
    2. Connect your Gmail, Google Calendar, or other vital tools.
    
    Now, your agent can execute:
    ```
    "RawBot, scan my recent emails. Are there any urgent messages from potential clients? If so, draft a polite reply asking them to schedule a call next week."
    ```
    
    <Check>
      **That's it.** You've successfully eliminated dashboard fatigue.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
