## The Complete Organizational OS

Individual founders use Anyx to manage their private workflows. Organizations use **Anyx for Business** to manage entire departments.

When you upgrade to Anyx for Business, you pivot from using generalized AI assistants to managing a fleet of highly specialized, context-aware AI employees that sit securely within your corporate network.

---

## 🏢 Centralized Team Deployment

Deploying AI safely across a company requires control.

### The Private Integration Hub
Instead of employees connecting their personal Google accounts individually, Anyx for Business provides a centralized **Integration Hub**. 

Your IT admins authorize corporate tools (Salesforce, Zendesk, internal databases) at the organizational level. Employees are then granted scoped, RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) permissions to access these tools via WhatsApp, ensuring data compliance without friction.

### Agent Fleet Management
Create custom-trained Groopy and Bizy agents specialized for different departments.
- **Support-Groopy:** Added to client WhatsApp groups to automatically resolve L1 support tickets by querying your Zendesk documentation.
- **Sales-Groopy:** Scans internal inventory databases and generates CRM quotes for field reps dynamically.

---

## 🔒 Enterprise Security & VPC

We understand that feeding corporate data into WhatsApp sounds terrifying. That's why Anyx for Business is built with military-grade isolation.

- **VPC Deployment:** Anyx can be deployed directly into your own AWS/GCP Virtual Private Cloud. We process the WhatsApp Webhooks, but the LLM states, databases, and memory clusters reside entirely within your boundary.
- **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK):** Retain total cryptographic control over your data.
- **PII Scrubbing:** Ensure sensitive customer data never touches a public LLM via our intermediate masking layer.
