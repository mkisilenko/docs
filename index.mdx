## What is Anyx?

Anyx is not a dashboard, a no-code builder, or another app you have to check. Anyx is the **first WorkOS native to WhatsApp**. 

Instead of managing tasks across 10 different platforms, you **delegate** them to autonomous AI agents—**RawBot** (your personal executive assistant) and **Groopy** (your team's operational manager)—using plain English voice notes or text messages.

No dashboards to refresh. No integrations to configure manually. Just chat and operate.

## The Death of Dashboards

Modern work is broken. We spend more time *managing* work in dashboards (Jira, Monday, Salesforce) than actually *doing* work. 

Anyx solves this through **Zero-Knowledge Delegation**:
1. You connect your apps via our secure Integration Hub.
2. You open WhatsApp.
3. You tell your agent what to do.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Inbox Triage">
    **"Read the noise so I don't have to."**
    
    RawBot flags urgent emails, summarizes long threads, and drafts your replies—directly from WhatsApp.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Autonomous Scheduling">
    **"Find 15 minutes with Sarah tomorrow."**
