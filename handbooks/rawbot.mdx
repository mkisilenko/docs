## Meet Your New Assistant

**RawBot** is your private, highly confidential, WorkOS agent on WhatsApp. It lives in a 1-on-1 chat with you and is designed to handle logistics, triage your digital life, and manage your immediate schedule.

If it takes less than 15 minutes to do, but you hate doing it—RawBot should be doing it.

---

## ☀️ The Morning Routine

Many founders use RawBot to act as the ultimate executive filter at the start of their day. 

Instead of opening your laptop and getting overwhelmed by 50 emails and Slack notifications, you manage your triage from bed, via WhatsApp.

### The Playbook: "The Daily Brief"

**1. Create the Briefing Rule**
Send this message to RawBot on WhatsApp:
```
"Every weekday morning at 7:30 AM, scan my integrated Gmail and Calendar. 
Give me a bulleted summary of:
1. Urgent emails I need to respond to today.
2. My meetings for the day.
3. Any unresolved tasks from yesterday."
```

**2. Triage via Voice Note**
When you receive your brief at 7:30 AM, review it over coffee. Then, hold down the microphone button on WhatsApp and send a voice note:
```
"Okay, tell David we can't do the meeting at 2 PM, ask him to reschedule for tomorrow morning. For the invoice email from Acme Corp, draft a reply saying payment will be processed by EOD. Skip the rest."
```

**3. Execution**
RawBot processes your voice note, connects to your Gmail, drafts the replies, checks your calendar, and handles the logistics without you ever opening the apps.

<Check>
  **Result:** You've handled 45 minutes of inbox anxiety in a 30-second voice note.
</Check>

## 🔍 Deep Research & Web Analysis

RawBot has full access to the live internet. Use it to prepare for meetings or summarize dense information.

**The "Pre-Meeting" Playbook:**
```
"I have a meeting with Acme Corp in 20 minutes. Read their website (acmecorp.com) and summarize their main value proposition, their primary competitors, and list 3 smart questions I should ask their sales director."
```

## 🧠 Managing Your Brain (Mem0)

RawBot is powered by **Mem0**, a stateful memory layer. It remembers what you tell it indefinitely. You can offload unstructured facts and retrieve them months later.

**The "Brain Dump" Playbook:**
```
"Remember that my accountant's email is jane@finance-inc.com and she needs my receipts sent as PDFs, not JPEGs."
```

Three months later:
```
"Can you email my latest Stripe invoice to my accountant? Use that preference she asked for."
```
RawBot autonomously retrieves her email, converts the file to a PDF, and sends it.
